About Us

Gifted Hands Independent Living Program is a faith-based transitional housing and independent living program dedicated to helping individuals rebuild their lives. We serve individuals overcoming homelessness, domestic violence, incarceration, and other life challenges while providing structure, accountability, and spiritual development.
